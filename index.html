Hey there, thanks for visiting. I am a Cleveland, Ohio native and live in Boulder, Colorado. My weekday life is centered around data and my weekend life is centered around mountains.

On the weekends I try to maximize time in the mountains: running, mountain biking, bike touring, skiing, climbing or scrambling.
